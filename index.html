About Me

Not just a developer.
A brand in motion.

I'm Amani Jordan, a 19-year-old self-taught Front-End Developer and digital creator. Originally from the Democratic Republic of the Congo and raised in Uganda, my journey has taught me resilience, adaptability, and the value of continuous learning. I discovered programming through curiosity and quickly realized it was more than a skill—it was an opportunity to create, solve problems, and build a future. With HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I turn ideas into modern, responsive, and user-friendly websites that prioritize performance and great user experience. Beyond coding, I'm building Amani Jordan Dev—a personal brand dedicated to sharing my journey, inspiring aspiring developers, and proving that talent can grow anywhere with consistency and determination. Every project I build is another step toward becoming a world-class developer and creating digital experiences that make a real impact. I'm not just writing code—I'm building a career, a brand, and a legacy.
